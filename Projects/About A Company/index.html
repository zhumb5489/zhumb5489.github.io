Welcome to Next-Level Sabers

Next-Level Sabers is a icon in the saber community. We have a wide range of both replica and custom sabers that will make any STAR-WARS fan's collection Next-Level! All our sabers come with smooth-swing and flash on clash blades,with the option to upgrade to higher sound chips and neopixel blades.

What is a neopixel blade?

Neopixel blades are some of the most cutting edge saber technology on the market.Instead of a light at the bottom of the saber creating the entire color of the blade, a neopixel has led lights throughout the blade giving the user more control over how the blade looks,and allowing the blade to activate in unique ways. This creates a lightsaber experience that is more movie accurate and gives the saber a more realistic activation.
